Q & A

Most prominant type of Cancer in men
Prostate Cancer, About 1 in 8 men will get prostate cancer in their lifetime. It can happen at any age, but the chances go up as a man gets older.
Most prominant type of Cancer in women
Breast Cancer, About 1 in 8 women will get invasive breast cancer in their lifetime.

Percent of kids with Cancer
Approximately 1 in 285 children in the U.S. will be diagnosed with cancer before their 20th birthday.
Treatements for Cancer
Surgery, Chemotherapy, Radiation Therapy, Targeted Therapy, Immunotherapy, Stem Cell or Bone Marrow Transplant, and Hormone Therapy
Symptoms of Cancer/ Early signs that you might have cancer
Some symptoms of Cancer include but are not limited to; Fatigue, Lump or area of thickening that can be felt under the skin, Weight changes including unintended loss or gain,Skin changes, such as yellowing, darkening or redness of the skin, sores that won't heal, changes to existing moles,Changes in bowel or bladder habits,Persistent cough or trouble breathing,
